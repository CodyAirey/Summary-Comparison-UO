Esther, Allan, Mr. Jarndyce, and the Bagnets visit George in prison and are dismayed at his refusal to have a lawyer (he wants his own innocence, not legal maneuverings, to clear his name). He watches Esther closely as she leaves, then tells Mr. Jarndyce that on the night of the murder, a figure like hers went past him on the dark staircase.
